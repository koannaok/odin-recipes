Burger Recipe

Description

Fuck macdonalds and burgerking. With this recipe you'll actually discover what a real burger tastes like. Go ahead and grab the required ingredients.

You can also go for a chicken-version of the burger if you'd rather eat chicken.

Ingredients

  • 100 grams of beef
  • 200 grams of pickles
  • Cheddar cheese
  • A bit of cream or whatever
  • Some random spices and herbs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat.
  2. Throw spices on the whole thing.
  3. Put the whole thing in the oven.
  4. When it looks crispy on the top, eat the whole plate and go to sleep.
